## 📌 Project Overview
**PitchSide Pro** is a comprehensive, 4-page interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze 5 years of global football e-commerce data. The project tracks consumer demand patterns, revenue streams, and purchasing habits across multiple merchandise categories—ranging from national club kits and training equipment to major global tournament apparel like the FIFA World Cup.

The primary goal of this analysis is to identify key macroeconomic drivers, regional sales performance, and seasonal trends to optimize inventory and maximize digital channel revenue growth.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Architecture & Insights

The report consists of 4 highly focused pages tailored to different business stakeholders:

1. **Overview**  
   * Provides a macro-level executive summary of global sales performance, total net revenue, profit margins, and baseline KPIs.

2. **Time Performance**  
   * Tracks performance velocity and revenue patterns over the 5-year timeline across years, quarters, months, and days to uncover historical anomalies and growth trajectories.

3. **Demographics & Category Sales**  
   * Deep-dives into geographic demand (by country and macro-region) and product category performance to pinpoint exactly which fan base and gear drives the highest volume.

4. **Channels & Seasons**  
   * Examines digital acquisition channels (Organic Search, Direct, Email, etc.) and isolates major seasonal peaks, proving the monumental revenue spike triggered during World Cup cycles.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Features & Core Functionalities

* **Interactive Buttons & Bookmarks:** Implemented custom navigation panels, hidden filter states, and interactive dynamic "Executive Insights" pop-up panels that preserve report real estate.

* **Data Modeling & DAX:** Applied star-schema design principles and utilized Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) to calculate exact revenue streams, net margins, and time-intelligence shifts.

* **User-Centric Visual Design:** Formatted with a highly styled, cohesive sports-commerce theme featuring conditional tooltips, customized global filter panels, and clear cross-filtering paths.
